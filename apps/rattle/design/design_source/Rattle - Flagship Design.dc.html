the pattern, and how it degrades.
LEARN WHERE YOU PLAY
A modal explains in the abstract; a coach mark points at the actual bead in the actual pile. The player's eye is already where their finger goes next — the rule sticks because it's spatial, not verbal.
THE BEATS
1 board loads, dims · spotlight lands on the new bead
2 bubble names it + one-line rule
3 a scripted cue fires the mechanic once (a nearby pair pops → the bomb bursts)
4 tap anywhere → dim lifts, taps counter live, you play
