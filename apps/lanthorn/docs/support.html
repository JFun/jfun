Frequently asked

How do I play?

Drag a piece from the tray onto the board. Fill a complete row or column that passes through a lantern to light it. Light every lantern to finish the level. That's the whole game — no timers, no accounts, no purchases.

Can I play offline?

Yes. Lanthorn is fully playable with no internet connection.

How do I reset my progress?

Progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app starts a fresh journey from Level 1.

Is there an account, login, or in-app purchase?

No. There's nothing to sign up for and nothing to buy.

What data does the app collect?

Only anonymous gameplay analytics, used to improve the levels. Details are in our Privacy Policy.
