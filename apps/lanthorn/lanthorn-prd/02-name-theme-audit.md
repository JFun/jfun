**Why lanterns wins:** goal state is self-evident on sight (dim lantern → lit lantern floats into the sky), gorgeous in a 3-second ad, cozy/broad appeal, glow effects = cheap juice for a solo artist, and the meta (your night sky fills with released lanterns) is a collection layer with zero economy.
