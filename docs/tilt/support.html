Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Tap the tray, then tilt your phone in any direction — the marbles roll with real gravity and momentum. Land each colored ball in the hole of the same color. Roll too slowly into a wrong-colored hole and the ball gets stuck; tilt hard to pop it free. Clear every ball to finish the level, and race the clock for three stars, across 30 levels. No accounts, no sign-in — just tilt and play.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Tilt is fully playable without an internet connection. Your progress is saved on your device.
How do I reset my progress?
Delete and reinstall the app to start over. All progress is stored locally on your device, so a fresh install gives you a clean slate.
How do I turn off sound or vibration?
Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to open Settings, where you can toggle Sound Effects and Vibration.
Are there ads or in-app purchases?
No. Tilt has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.
Is the game suitable for kids?
Yes — it's a calm, non-violent puzzle with no chat, no social features, and no purchases.
