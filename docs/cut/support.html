Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Swipe your finger across a rope to cut it, then use gravity, swing, and momentum to land the wooden crate in the basket. Later levels add pulleys, balloons, sliding hooks, timed gates, springy cords, spinning anchors, sawblades, wind, magnets, and stars to collect — each one is introduced with a short wordless demo the first time you meet it. 53 handcrafted levels, and no accounts or sign-in — just swipe and play.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Cut is fully playable without an internet connection. Your progress is saved on your device.
How do I see the tutorials again?
Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner and choose How to play for a paged walkthrough of every mechanic, any time.
How do I reset my progress?
Delete and reinstall the app to start over. All progress is stored locally on your device, so a fresh install gives you a clean slate.
How do I turn off sound or vibration?
Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to open Settings, where you can toggle Sound Effects and Vibration.
Are there ads or in-app purchases?
No. Cut has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.
Is the game suitable for kids?
Yes — it's a calm, non-violent puzzle with no chat, no social features, and no purchases.
