Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Tap any group of two or more touching beads of the same colour to pop them. The pile then falls and re-clusters under gravity, setting up your next move. Each level gives you a limited number of taps and a goal — clear a colour, crack the crates, pop the balloons, or bring the duck down to the floor. Later levels add stones, crates, balloons, and bombs, each introduced the first time you meet it. 106 handcrafted levels, and no accounts or sign-in — just tap and play.
I'm stuck — there are no groups to tap.
You're never truly stuck. Tap any empty space to rattle the jar: the whole pile shakes and re-settles into fresh clusters. A rattle costs one tap, and the game will offer it automatically if no pair is left.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Rattle is fully playable without an internet connection, and it collects no data. Your progress is saved on your device.
How do I turn off sound or vibration?
Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to open Settings, where you can toggle Sound and Haptics.
How do I restart a level or reset my progress?
Open Settings (the gear, top-right) and tap Restart to replay the current level, or Reset save to clear all levels and stars and start over. Deleting and reinstalling the app also gives you a clean slate.
Are there ads or in-app purchases?
No. Rattle has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.
Is the game suitable for kids?
Yes — it's a calm, non-violent puzzle with no chat, no social features, and no purchases.
